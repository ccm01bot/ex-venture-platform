# AI News: The Last 48 Hours

* **Google Cloud Next 2026 — Massive AI Infrastructure and Agent Platform Announcements:** Google held its flagship **Cloud Next '26** conference in Las Vegas (April 22-24), dropping a wave of announcements. **Vertex AI has evolved into the Gemini Enterprise Agent Platform** — a unified platform to build, scale, govern, and optimize AI agents. It includes a new **Agent Designer** for natural-language agent creation, an **Agent Studio** low-code builder, a simulation environment for stress-testing agents, and a marketplace for third-party agents. Google also announced **$750 million** in partner funding to accelerate agentic AI development across its 120,000-member partner ecosystem. [Source: https://cloud.google.com/blog/products/ai-machine-learning/introducing-gemini-enterprise-agent-platform]

* **Google — Two New TPU Chips for the Agentic Era:** Google unveiled its **eighth-generation TPUs** with a first-ever dual-chip approach: **TPU 8t** optimized for training and **TPU 8i** optimized for inference. TPU 8t scales up to **9,600 TPUs** in a single superpod with **2 petabytes** of shared high-bandwidth memory. TPU 8i features **3x more SRAM** than the previous-gen Ironwood for faster LLM text generation. Both deliver up to **2x better performance-per-watt** over the previous generation. [Source: https://blog.google/innovation-and-ai/infrastructure-and-cloud/google-cloud/eighth-generation-tpu-agentic-era/]

* **Google — Gemma 4 Open Models Released:** Google launched **Gemma 4**, its most capable open models ever, under **Apache 2.0 license**. Available in four sizes — **E2B, E4B, 26B MoE, and 31B Dense** — the 31B model ranks **#3 on the Arena AI text leaderboard** and outperforms models **20x its size** on reasoning benchmarks. Features include **256K context windows**, native vision and audio processing, fluency in **140+ languages**, and strong agentic workflow capabilities. Over **400 million downloads** of Gemma models to date. [Source: https://blog.google/innovation-and-ai/technology/developers-tools/gemma-4/]

* **Google — Workspace Intelligence Launches:** Google introduced **Workspace Intelligence**, a new semantic layer that maps emails, chats, files, collaborators, and projects into shared context for Gemini-powered agents. New features include **"Ask Gemini in Chat"** as a unified command line for work, daily briefings that surface urgent tasks, **Skills** for building automated workflows across Workspace, an **inbox assistant in Gmail**, and an **agentic builder in Google Slides**. [Source: https://workspace.google.com/blog/product-announcements/introducing-workspace-intelligence]

* **Google — Chrome Gets Gemini Auto Browse for Enterprise:** Google integrated **Gemini agents into Chrome** via **Auto Browse**, enabling enterprise users to automate web tasks like booking travel, inputting CRM data, comparing prices across tabs, and extracting data from competitor pages. All automation stays under human control — users must review and approve actions before execution. [Source: https://blog.google/products-and-platforms/products/chrome/gemini-3-auto-browse/]

* **OpenAI — ChatGPT Images 2.0 Launches:** OpenAI released **ChatGPT Images 2.0** on April 21, featuring native reasoning ("thinking") before generating, **2K resolution**, multi-image consistency, and vastly improved **in-image text rendering** — menus, posters, and UI mock-ups now render readable text instead of gibberish. The model can search the web, make multiple images from one prompt, and double-check its own creations. Available to **all ChatGPT users** including Free tier, with advanced outputs for Plus and Pro. Also available via the API and Codex. [Source: https://openai.com/index/introducing-chatgpt-images-2-0/]

* **OpenAI — GPT-Rosalind Life Sciences Model:** OpenAI introduced **GPT-Rosalind**, a frontier reasoning model built specifically for **biology, drug discovery, and translational medicine**. Named after Rosalind Franklin, it's optimized for genomics, proteomics, molecular biology, and drug discovery workflows. Available as a **research preview** through a Trusted Access program for qualified Enterprise customers in the US, with partners including **Amgen, Moderna, the Allen Institute, and Thermo Fisher Scientific**. Also launched a free **Life Sciences research plugin for Codex** connecting to 50+ scientific tools and data sources. [Source: https://openai.com/index/introducing-gpt-rosalind/]

* **OpenAI — Codex Major Desktop Update:** OpenAI shipped a broad Codex update on April 16, adding **background computer use** on macOS (seeing, clicking, and typing with its own cursor), an **in-app browser** for commenting on pages with precise instructions, **image generation** with gpt-image-1.5 inside coding workflows, **memory** for suggesting how to start your work day, and **90+ new plugins** including Atlassian Rovo, CircleCI, CodeRabbit, GitLab Issues, Microsoft Suite, Neon by Databricks, Remotion, and Render. Used by over **3 million developers weekly**. [Source: https://openai.com/index/codex-for-almost-everything/]

* **Anthropic — Claude Opus 4.7 Released:** Anthropic released **Claude Opus 4.7** on April 16, generally available across all Claude products, the API, Amazon Bedrock, Google Cloud Vertex AI, and Microsoft Foundry. Scores **87.6% on SWE-bench Verified** and **94.2% on GPQA**. Substantially improved vision with higher resolution, and better performance on complex long-running agent tasks. Pricing stays at **$5/$25 per million input/output tokens**. Ships with a new **Cyber Verification Program** for legitimate cybersecurity use. [Source: https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-opus-4-7]

* **Anthropic — Claude for Word Beta Expands:** Anthropic's **Claude for Word** add-in, launched April 11, is now available on **Claude Pro, Max, Team, and Enterprise plans** as of April 22. The native sidebar integration for Microsoft Word features document awareness, **tracked changes** for all edits, and cross-document conversation spanning **Word, Excel, and PowerPoint** simultaneously. Particularly strong for legal review, financial memo drafting, and detailed editing. [Source: https://www.thurrott.com/a-i/334834/anthropic-launches-claude-for-word-in-beta]

* **TwelveLabs — Pegasus 1.5 Video Intelligence Model at NAB Show:** TwelveLabs launched **Pegasus 1.5** at NAB Show 2026, introducing **Time-Based Metadata Extraction (TBM)** — the first model to discover temporal boundaries in video and extract structured metadata to a custom JSON schema from videos up to **2 hours long**, with no preprocessing or indexing. Outperforms **Gemini 2.5 Pro by 30%** on segmentation quality benchmarks. Already in production with a major broadcast network. Also introduced **Rodeo**, an AI creative co-pilot, and a partnership with **Autodesk** for digital dailies workflows. [Source: https://www.twelvelabs.io/blog/introducing-pegasus-1-5]

* **LINE Yahoo — Agent i Unified AI Agent:** LINE Yahoo launched **Agent i** on April 20, a unified AI agent brand consolidating Yahoo! JAPAN AI Assistant and LINE AI into a single service. Offers **seven domain agents** covering shopping, outings, weather, automobiles, relationships, work, and recipes. Reaches **94% of smartphone users in Japan** through LINE's 98 million and Yahoo! JAPAN's 89 million monthly active users. Plans to expand to **20+ domains by September**. [Source: https://itbusinesstoday.com/tech/ai/line-yahoo-launches-new-ai-agent-brand-agent-i/]

* **Meta — Muse Spark Proprietary AI Model:** Meta launched **Muse Spark** on April 8, the first model from its new **Muse series** developed by Meta Superintelligence Labs. This is Meta's first major AI model since bringing in Scale AI's **Alexandr Wang**. Unlike the open-source Llama series, Muse Spark launches as a **proprietary model** with competitive performance in multimodal perception, reasoning, health, and agentic tasks. Meta claims improved training techniques enable models as capable as older Llama 4 variants for **"an order of magnitude less compute."** [Source: https://venturebeat.com/technology/goodbye-llama-meta-launches-new-proprietary-ai-model-muse-spark-first-since]

* **ICLR 2026 — World's Top AI Research Conference Kicks Off:** The **14th International Conference on Learning Representations (ICLR 2026)** opened in **Rio de Janeiro, Brazil** on April 23, running through April 27. **Microsoft** has over **150 accepted papers**, **Apple** is presenting research on large-scale RNN training, state space models, unified image understanding, 3D scene generation, and protein folding. [Source: https://iclr.cc]
